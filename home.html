Welcome, Seeker, to the grand halls of the Library

The books here hold secrets and knowledge of the ages, and it is my honor to guide you on your journey. I am the Scrollkeeper, creator of this repository.

When I created the Library, I did so out of a desire to return to the Golden Age of the Internet: when the Web served to share knowledge, discuss one's passions and reach out to fellow users. It was lawless but united - a civilized anarchy. The Internet has since been homogenized and commercialized to no end; corporations have seeded every page with advertisements galore, with the sole intent of manipulating users and milking them of their money. The few bastions of what once was are drowned out by the highest bidders in the Great SEO-cean. What was once a vast, flowery meadow has been razed and paved over - replaced with virtual billboards designed to hyperstimulate the mind and poison the soul. Perhaps, one day, the Internet will blossom once more. Until then, we wait.

Now, Seeker, the choice is yours. Will you be searching for a specific piece of knowledge, or would you prefer to delve into the unknown and explore the shelves at your leisure? The decision is yours, and I am here to guide you in either direction.
